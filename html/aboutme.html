Football

I owe who I am today to football. Ever since I was young, all I wanted to do was play football. I worked day in and day out to be the best ever. Even though I was shorter and smaller than everyone else on that field, I worked so hard to achieve my dream. After my senior year of high school and countless football camps, I recieved football scholarships to all over the country. I decided to take a offer in Virginia to pursue my dream. It was because of my dedication, motivation and desire that I achieved all I did in football and all sports I participated in.

    Accolades

  • 4 time letterman in football, soccer and track
  • 23 scholarship offers
  • outstanding player awarded at University of Utah elite camp
  • top 5 long jumper, triple jumper and top 3 sprinter in the state of Hawaii.
